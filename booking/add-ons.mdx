## When to use each add-on

- **Inside fridge and inside oven** are a natural fit alongside a Deep Clean or Move-in/out service, when you want every surface in the kitchen thoroughly addressed.
- **HVAC vent & register wipe** helps with air quality and is especially useful if you haven't had vents wiped down in a while.
- **Interior windows** and **blind / window track detail** are good choices when you want the full picture cleaned, not just the sills.
- **Baseboards** and **wall spot-cleaning** are useful after a long winter or before hosting guests.
- **Pet hair surcharge** lets your crew member bring the extra time and tools needed to manage pet hair throughout the home.
- **Patio / balcony** and **garage sweep & tidy** extend the clean to outdoor-adjacent spaces.
- **Debris haul-away** covers removal of construction or renovation materials — pair it with a Post-Construction clean for a complete result.

## Add-ons on recurring bookings

When you set up a recurring schedule, any add-ons you select during setup are applied to every clean in your schedule automatically. You'll see the per-visit price with add-ons included before you confirm the recurring plan.

<Tip>
  Use the notes field on your booking to flag a specific area that needs extra attention — for example, a particularly grimy stovetop or a closet that needs a quick vacuum. Your crew member will see the note before they arrive.
</Tip>
