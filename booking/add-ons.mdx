## When to use each add-on

- **Inside fridge and inside oven** are a natural fit alongside a [Deep Clean](/services/deep-clean) or [Move-in / out](/services/move-in-out) service, when you want every surface in the kitchen thoroughly addressed.
- **HVAC vents & registers** helps with air quality and is especially useful if you haven't had vents wiped down in a while.
- **Interior windows** and **blinds / window-track detail** are good choices when you want the full picture cleaned, not just the sills.
- **Baseboards** and **wall spot-clean** are useful after a long winter or before hosting guests.
- **Pet-hair detail** lets your Pro bring the extra time and tools needed to manage pet hair throughout the home.
- **Patio / balcony** and **garage sweep & tidy** extend the clean to outdoor-adjacent spaces.
- **Debris haul-away** covers removal of construction or renovation materials. Pair it with a [Post-construction](/services/post-construction) clean for a complete result.

## Add-ons on recurring bookings

When you set up a recurring schedule, any add-ons you select during setup are applied to every clean in your schedule automatically. You'll see the per-visit price with add-ons included before you confirm the recurring plan.

<Tip>
  Use the notes field on your booking to flag a specific area that needs extra attention. For example, a particularly grimy stovetop or a closet that needs a quick vacuum. Your Pro will see the note before they arrive.
</Tip>
