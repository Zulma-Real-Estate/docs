---
title: "How to book a Zulma home cleaning or lawn care job"
description: "Book a one-time clean or lawn job on app.zulma.ca in minutes — choose your service, configure your home, pick a time, and get matched."
---

Booking a Zulma service takes only a few minutes. Visit [app.zulma.ca](https://app.zulma.ca), pick your service, configure your home, and choose a time slot. Your card is pre-authorized but not charged until a crew member accepts — and matching typically takes under 30 seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a service">
    Go to [app.zulma.ca](https://app.zulma.ca) and select the service you need:

    - **Standard Clean** — regular upkeep for a fresh home (from $169)
    - **Deep Clean** — top-to-bottom, every corner (from $256)
    - **Move-in / out** — complete clean for transitions (from $297)
    - **Post-Construction** — remove dust and debris after a build (from $338)
    - **Lawn care** — mowing, seasonal cleanups, hedge trim, and more
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your home">
    Select the number of bedrooms and bathrooms so Zulma can quote you accurately. Then choose any add-ons you want included — each one adjusts your quoted price in real time before you confirm. Add-ons are entirely optional.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a date and time slot">
    Choose **Today** for same-day service or pick a future date to schedule ahead. Available time slots are:

    **8:00 AM · 10:00 AM · 11:00 AM · 12:30 PM · 2:00 PM · 3:30 PM**

    <Note>
      Same-day bookings made less than 24 hours in advance include a **$50 rush fee**, shown transparently on your quote before you confirm.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your home details and access notes">
    Enter your home address and select your property type (house, townhouse, condo, or apartment). Then add any notes your crew member should know before they arrive — all fields are optional but helpful:

    - **Property access** — lockbox code, smart lock code, gate code, or parking instructions
    - **Pets & people** — let the crew know if pets will be home
    - **Kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms** — any specific room requests
    - **Mobile number** — add your number to receive ETA texts (used only for this booking, no marketing)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your price and continue to payment">
    Before you confirm, review the full price breakdown:

    - Base price (based on service, bedrooms, and bathrooms)
    - Any add-ons you selected
    - Same-day rush fee (+$50, if applicable)
    - HST (13%)

    When you're ready, tap **Continue to payment**. Your card is pre-authorized for the quoted amount — it is not charged until a crew member accepts the job.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get matched">
    After you confirm, Zulma matches you with an available, vetted crew member. Average match time is under 30 seconds in the Niagara core service area. You'll be notified as soon as someone accepts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You only pay when a crew member accepts. If no one accepts your booking, your pre-authorization is fully released and nothing is charged to your card.
</Note>

<Tip>
  After a great visit, save that crew member to your favorites. You can re-book the same person directly from your home screen or from [account/favorites](https://app.zulma.ca/account/favorites), and Zulma will match them to your next booking whenever they're available.
</Tip>
