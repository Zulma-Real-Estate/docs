A recurring schedule means you never have to think about booking again. Set your preferred service, frequency, and time once, and Zulma handles the rest: the same Pro whenever possible, the same time slot, every visit. You can skip, pause, or cancel at any time with no penalty.

## Discount tiers

More frequent visits earn a larger discount per clean. The discount applies from your second visit onward, your first clean is at the standard rate.

| Frequency | Cleans per month | Discount per clean |
|---|---|---|
| Every week | 4 | 20% off |
| Every 2 weeks *(most popular)* | 2 | 15% off |
| Monthly | 1 | 10% off |

<Info>
  Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch, so your price is all-in. The number you see at checkout is the number you pay.
</Info>

## Set up your recurring schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recurring setup page">
    Go to [your Zulma account](https://app.zulma.ca), or tap **Set up recurring** from your home screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your service type">
    Select the kind of clean you want on every visit. Starting prices for a 3-bed, 2-bath home:

    - **Standard Clean** from \$219
    - **Deep Clean** from \$315
    - **Move-in / out** from \$347
    - **Post-construction** from \$429

    These are "from" prices. You'll see your exact per-visit quote once you enter your home details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your home details">
    Select the number of bedrooms and bathrooms. Zulma uses this to generate an accurate per-visit quote with the recurring discount already applied.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose add-ons">
    Add any optional services you want included on every clean, for example inside fridge, baseboards, or a pet-hair detail. Any add-ons you select here are applied to every visit in your schedule. See [add-on pricing](/booking/add-ons) for the full list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your frequency">
    Pick how often you want your home cleaned: weekly, every 2 weeks, or monthly. Your discount tier updates as you choose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your preferred day and time slot">
    Choose a day of the week (Mon to Sun) and a time slot. Your recurring clean will land on the same day each cycle.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter or select your address">
    Choose a saved address or enter a new one. This is the address Zulma will clean on every recurring visit.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a preferred Pro (optional)">
    If you have a favourite Pro, select them here. Zulma will match them to your recurring visits whenever they're available. Leave this set to **Match me with anyone** if you want the fastest possible matching with any vetted Zulma Pro.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add standing notes">
    Enter any notes that apply to every visit: access codes, pet information, parking instructions, or room-specific requests. These notes carry over to each clean automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Skip, pause, or cancel

Life happens, and your schedule flexes with it. There's no penalty for changing a recurring plan.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Skip a single visit">
    Going away for a week or simply don't need this one? Skip an upcoming clean from your schedule and the rest of the series stays exactly as it is. Your discount tier is unaffected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pause the whole schedule">
    Pause your recurring plan when you'll be away for a while. No visits are generated and nothing is charged while you're paused. Resume whenever you're ready and your settings, frequency, preferred Pro, and standing notes, are all still there.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cancel anytime">
    Cancel the schedule outright with no penalty. Any visit already in progress or a Pro already on site follows the standard [cancellation policy](/booking/cancellation), but future visits simply stop.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Want to change your service, frequency, or add-ons? You can adjust your recurring settings at any time, and the new rate applies from your next visit. Your discount always reflects your current frequency.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Prefer to book one clean at a time? See [how to book](/booking/how-to-book) for a single visit, then convert to recurring later if you love it.
</Tip>
