---
title: "Set up recurring Zulma cleaning and save up to 20%"
description: "Schedule weekly, biweekly, or monthly cleans with the same crew member and lock in a discounted rate — skip, pause, or cancel anytime."
---

A recurring schedule means you never have to think about booking again. Set your preferred service, frequency, and time once, and Zulma handles the rest — same crew member whenever possible, same time slot, every visit. You can skip, pause, or cancel at any time with no penalty.

## Discount tiers

More frequent visits earn a larger discount per clean:

| Frequency | Cleans per month | Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Every week | 4 | 20% off per clean |
| Every 2 weeks *(most popular)* | 2 | 15% off per clean |
| Monthly | 1 | 10% off per clean |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recurring setup page">
    Go to [account/recurring/new](https://app.zulma.ca/account/recurring/new), or tap **Set up recurring** from your home screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your service type">
    Select the kind of clean you want on every visit:

    - **Standard Clean** — from $169
    - **Deep Clean** — from $256
    - **Move-in / out** — from $297
    - **Post-Construction** — from $338
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your home details">
    Select the number of bedrooms and bathrooms. Zulma uses this to generate an accurate per-visit quote with the recurring discount already applied.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose add-ons">
    Add any optional services you want included on every clean — for example, inside fridge, baseboards, or pet hair surcharge. Any add-ons you select here are applied to every visit in your recurring schedule.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your frequency">
    Pick how often you want your home cleaned: weekly, every 2 weeks, or monthly. Your discount tier updates as you choose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your preferred day and time slot">
    Choose a day of the week (Mon–Sun) and a time slot. Your recurring clean will land on the same day each cycle. Available time slots are:

    **8:00 AM · 10:00 AM · 11:00 AM · 12:30 PM · 2:00 PM · 3:30 PM**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter or select your address">
    Choose a saved address or enter a new one. This is the address Zulma will clean on every recurring visit.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a preferred crew member (optional)">
    If you have a favorite crew member, select them here. Zulma will match them to your recurring visits whenever they're available. Leave this set to **Match me with anyone** if you want the fastest possible matching with any vetted Zulma crew member.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add standing notes">
    Enter any notes that apply to every visit — access codes, pet information, parking instructions, or room-specific requests. These notes carry over to each clean automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your recurring price is locked for the duration of your schedule. If a region-wide rate adjustment is ever necessary, Zulma will give you at least 30 days' notice before it takes effect. You can cancel or pause your schedule at any time with no penalty.
</Note>
