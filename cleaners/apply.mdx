The application takes about five minutes online. From there, Zulma reviews your application within two business days and walks you through the rest. Most cleaners are earning within two weeks of submitting their application.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply online">
    Go to [app.zulma.ca/signup?role=pro](https://app.zulma.ca/signup?role=pro) and fill out the application — it takes about five minutes. Tell Zulma about your experience, the specific Niagara communities you want to serve, and your availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Background check + WHMIS certification">
    Zulma will initiate a **vulnerable-sector criminal record check** on your behalf. You also complete **WHMIS 2015 chemical-safety certification** — both are provided free of charge. No criminal record check fees come out of your pocket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your profile and complete onboarding">
    Once cleared, you build your public crew profile — the name, photo, and bio that customers see when they're matched with you. You then watch a 30-minute orientation video and have a brief call with your regional lead to go through expectations and the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go online and start accepting jobs">
    Toggle online in the app and begin receiving job offers in your area. You see the full payout breakdown — base pay, bonuses, and tips — before you accept anything. Most cleaners complete their first paid job within 48 hours of going live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect on your first booking

<Note>
Your first booking is shadowed by a senior Zulma crew member. You do not take solo jobs until you pass the skills check. This is standard for all new crew — it protects you and the customer, and most people find it useful to see how an experienced cleaner handles the job flow.
</Note>

## Timeline at a glance

| Stage | Typical timeline |
|---|---|
| Application review | Within 2 business days |
| Background check + WHMIS | 3–5 business days |
| Profile + onboarding | 1–2 days |
| First job | Within 48 hours of going live |
| **First paycheck** | **Under 2 weeks from application** |
