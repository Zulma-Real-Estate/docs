Becoming a Zulma Pro is a clear, step-by-step process. You apply, verify who you are, clear a background check, confirm you carry the required insurance, and once you're approved you accept jobs in the Pro app. Zulma is service-agnostic, so the same approval lets you take cleaning, lawn care, and car detailing work.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply online">
    Start your application at [app.zulma.ca/signup?role=pro](https://app.zulma.ca/signup?role=pro). Tell Zulma about your experience, the Niagara communities you want to serve, and your availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity and pass a background check">
    Every Pro is identity-verified and cleared through a third-party criminal record check before taking any jobs. This keeps the bar high for everyone on the platform and is what lets customers trust who shows up at their door.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your insurance">
    Pros carry their own liability insurance. You'll confirm your coverage during onboarding, before you're approved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved and accept jobs in the Pro app">
    Once you're approved, you start receiving job offers in the Pro app for your area. You see the full payout breakdown before you accept anything, and you can accept or pass on each offer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you'll need

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Identity verification">
    A government-issued ID so Zulma can confirm you are who you say you are.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A clear background check">
    Zulma runs a third-party criminal record check. You complete this before you can take jobs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Your own liability insurance">
    Pros carry their own coverage. Zulma also carries $5M commercial general liability and $100K cyber coverage at the platform level. See [insurance](/trust/insurance) for how the two layers fit together.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A payout account">
    Payouts run through your connected payout account, powered by Stripe. You set this up during onboarding so you can get paid for completed jobs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What you keep when you work

You keep **70% of the base, add-ons, and surcharges** on every job, plus **100% of any tip**. Recurring discounts come out of Zulma's share, not yours, so you're paid on the pre-discount amount. You always see the full payout breakdown before you accept a job.

For the complete breakdown, see [how pay works](/cleaners/how-pay-works).
