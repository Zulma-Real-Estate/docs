Base pay is the floor. The biggest extra you can earn on top of it is the Pro referral program: bring another Pro onto Zulma and you both get paid as they get going.

<Note>
Every referral bonus is paid automatically to your connected payout account, the same place your job earnings land. You never have to invoice anyone or chase a payment.
</Note>

## The Pro referral program

Refer another Pro to Zulma. When they join, get approved, and start completing jobs, bonuses unlock for both of you. It is two-sided: you earn, and so does the Pro you brought in.

The bonuses pay out in four steps as the referred Pro reaches Zulma-commission milestones. As they keep working and Zulma earns its commission on their jobs, each step unlocks:

| Milestone (referred Pro's Zulma commission) | Bonus per side |
|---|---|
| About \$100 | **\$25** |
| About \$300 | **\$50** |
| About \$600 | **\$75** |
| About \$1,000 | **\$50** |

That is **up to \$200 per side** once a referred Pro is fully established. Both you and the Pro you referred receive the same amounts at each step.

<Info>
The program is service-agnostic. It works the same whether the Pro you refer does cleaning, lawn care, or car detailing.
</Info>

A few details to keep in mind:

- You can refer **up to 12 Pros per year**.
- Bonuses are paid to your connected payout account, the same place your job earnings land.
- Each step unlocks as the referred Pro hits the matching milestone, so the full \$200 builds up over time as they stay active.

<Tip>
The best people to refer are Pros who will stick around and stay busy. The bonus is built to reward referrals who become active, reliable members of the platform, not one-time sign-ups.
</Tip>

## Recurring work builds steady income

The referral program rewards you for growing the Pro community. Recurring bookings reward you for doing great work that customers want again.

When a customer books a recurring schedule, weekly, biweekly, or monthly, the agreed per-visit price is locked for the duration of that schedule. That gives both you and the customer cost predictability, and it turns one good job into a steady stream of repeat work.

<Tip>
Recurring discounts come out of Zulma's share, not yours. You are paid on the full pre-discount amount for every visit, so a recurring customer is worth the same per visit to you as a one-off.
</Tip>

## How your earnings add up

Your base pay covers the work itself. The referral program rewards you for bringing strong new Pros onto the platform. Recurring bookings reward you for delivering work customers want again.
