Apex is the private division of Zulma, built for spaces where discretion matters as much as the clean. It's not on the public booking flow. You come to Apex by introduction or inquiry, and every detail is handled quietly.

## What Apex is

Apex runs differently from the rest of Zulma. The work is done by **W-2 employees**. Direct staff, not contractors. So the same vetted, accountable team returns to your space. It's designed for settings where privacy, predictability, and trust are non-negotiable:

- Executive offices and boardrooms
- High-net-worth private residences
- Luxury post-construction handovers

<Info>
Apex is **by introduction or inquiry only**. There's no self-serve booking. Every engagement starts with a conversation and a scoped site visit.
</Info>

## Who it's for

Apex suits environments that can't be handled like an ordinary account: places where the cleaning team needs to be discreet, screened, and consistent, and where the work is sensitive enough that "anyone available" isn't an option.

If your space requires a non-disclosure agreement, vetted personnel, and a single accountable point of contact, Apex is built for you. For standard commercial accounts. Offices, retail, salons, medical, restaurants. See our [commercial service tiers](/commercial/service-tiers) instead.

## The screening

Before anyone sets foot in your space, the Apex crew clears a heightened bar:

- **NDA-bound**. Every team member signs a non-disclosure agreement
- **Government-reliability screened**. Beyond a standard background check
- **$5M insured and bonded**
- **WSIB-cleared**

This screening happens **before any site visit**, so the first people you meet are already cleared to be there.
