No quotes-by-appointment-only. No mystery line items. Your price is published, your scope is written down, and your visits are documented.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a price from the calculator">
    Enter your square footage, industry, frequency, and tier at the [commercial pricing calculator](https://app.zulma.ca/commercial/pricing). Pricing is per square foot and published, so you see your number right away. No appointment required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive a written quote">
    We turn your inputs into a written quote with the scope, tier, frequency, and price spelled out. You can ask for a quick call before you sign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the service agreement">
    Approve the quote and clickwrap the agreement. Set your access method and any access notes, which are surfaced read-only to the assigned crew. Your first clean is often scheduled within a week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recurring service begins">
    Your crew arrives on schedule. Every visit is documented with photo proof and a sanitization log, and billing recurs monthly. Change frequency or tier anytime with 14 days' notice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tiers at a glance

Every account runs on one of three tiers. The tier sets both the depth of each visit and where you land in the per-square-foot range.

| Tier | What it covers | Where it sits |
|---|---|---|
| Essential | Core recurring upkeep: trash, floors, surfaces, restrooms | Floor of the per-sqft range |
| Professional | Essential plus glass, interior windows, and added detail | Middle of the range |
| Premium | Professional plus deep touch-point sanitization | Ceiling of the range |

<Tip>
  Not sure which tier fits? Start with [service tiers](/commercial/service-tiers) to compare them side by side, or jump straight to a quote and adjust from there.
</Tip>
