Every commercial account picks one of three service tiers: **Essential**, **Professional**, or **Premium**. The tier decides **how deep each visit goes**. And where you land in your industry's price range. Same square footage, same building: the tier is what changes.

## What each tier covers

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Essential">
    **Every visit:**
    - Trash and recycling collected and taken out
    - Vacuum carpet, sweep hard floors
    - Surface wipe-down (desks, counters, common areas)
    - Restroom sanitization (toilets, sinks, mirrors, restocking)

    **Not included**. Step up to Professional or Premium for:
    - Mopping hard floors
    - Glass and interior windows
    - Kitchen / break-room appliance exteriors
    - Detailed restroom and dusting work

    **Best for:** lower-traffic spaces on a tight budget, or light upkeep between deeper Professional/Premium passes.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Professional">
    **Everything in Essential, plus:**
    - Mop hard floors
    - Glass and interior windows
    - Kitchen / break-room appliance exteriors (microwave, fridge handles, coffee machine)
    - Detailed restrooms
    - Dusting of reachable surfaces

    **Best for:** most commercial accounts. This is the default. Roughly **70% of accounts** choose Professional.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Premium">
    **Everything in Professional, plus:**
    - Hospital-grade touch-point disinfection (door handles, light switches, shared keyboards, phones)
    - Green / ECOLOGO-certified products
    - Detailed dusting
    - Inside the microwave
    - A **per-visit photo and sanitization report**
    - Quarterly deep dives

    **Best for:** medical and dental clinics, client-facing offices, brand-forward retail, and any account that wants an auditable paper trail.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Which tier does my industry need?

Most industries can use any of the three tiers. A few have a sensible floor. For the scope the space genuinely needs. So we'll guide you there.

| Industry | Lowest tier we offer | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Offices | Essential | All three tiers fit. |
| Retail | Essential | All three tiers fit. |
| Salons / spas | Essential | All three fit; many choose Professional for the extra detail. |
| Restaurants | **Professional** | Essential isn't offered. Kitchens need the deeper scope. Kitchen work is already built into the per-sq-ft rate. |
| Medical / dental | **Premium recommended** | Essential isn't offered. Premium's documentation and disinfection are what clinics usually want. |

You can always choose a **higher** tier than the floor. For restaurants and medical/dental, Essential simply isn't on the menu.

<Note>
  The per-square-foot ranges and contract-length discounts that go with each tier live in [getting a quote](/commercial/getting-a-quote). Restaurants carry kitchen work inside the rate; medical/dental sits at the higher end of the scale.
</Note>

## Switching tiers mid-contract

Your needs change. Your tier can too.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Give us 14 days' notice">
    Email <a href="mailto:business@zulma.ca">business@zulma.ca</a> with the tier you'd like to move to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We reprice and prorate">
    We adjust your rate to the new tier and prorate the current month, so you only pay for what you used.
  </Step>
  <Step title="No re-contract">
    No new paperwork, no resetting your contract term or your contract-length discount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Thinking about downgrading? Try one visit at the lower tier first so you can see exactly what changes before you commit. Upgrading back is always easy.
</Tip>
