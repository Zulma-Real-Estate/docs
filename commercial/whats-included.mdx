Every commercial clean is scoped to your industry. An office, a restaurant, and a dental clinic each need a different checklist, so we build the scope around the space you actually run. This page lists what a visit covers in each industry, then shows how the service tier changes how deep that visit goes.

## How scope and tier work together

Two things shape your clean:

- **Your industry** sets the checklist below. The tasks a restaurant kitchen needs are different from a retail sales floor.
- **Your tier** sets how deep each visit goes. Same building, same square footage: the tier is what changes.

| Tier | What it adds |
|---|---|
| **Essential** | The basics. Trash, vacuum, sweep, surface wipe-down, restrooms. Sits at the floor of your industry's price range. |
| **Professional** | Essential plus mopping, glass and interior windows, appliance exteriors, and detailed restrooms. The most common pick. Sits in the middle of the range. |
| **Premium** | Professional plus deep touch-point sanitization and detailed dusting. Sits at the top of the range. |

<Info>
  Restaurants start at **Professional** (kitchens need the deeper scope). For medical and dental we recommend **Premium**. Full tier details, including which industries can use which tiers, are in [commercial service tiers](/commercial/service-tiers).
</Info>

## What each visit covers

Pick your industry below. These are the tasks built into the per-visit scope. Glass and interior windows come in at the Professional tier and up; deep touch-point sanitization is a Premium feature.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Offices">
    **Every visit:**
    - Trash and recycling collected and taken out
    - Vacuum carpet, sweep and mop hard floors
    - Surface wipe-down (desks, counters, common areas)
    - Dusting of reachable surfaces
    - Restroom sanitization (toilets, sinks, mirrors, restocking)
    - Kitchen and break-room tidy, including appliance exteriors

    **At Professional and up:**
    - Glass and interior windows

    **At Premium:**
    - Deep touch-point sanitization (door handles, light switches, shared keyboards, phones)

    **Best for:** any workspace, from a small studio to a multi-floor office. All three tiers fit.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Restaurants">
    Restaurants start at the **Professional** tier. Kitchen work is already priced into the rate.

    **Every visit:**
    - Front-of-house clean
    - Kitchen floor degrease and sanitize
    - Hood-area wipe and grease-trap exterior
    - Walk-in fridge and freezer floor plus door seals
    - Restroom sanitization
    - Glass, entrance, and mirrors
    - Trash, recycling, and organic-waste collection
    - Sanitization log plus per-visit photo proof

    <Note>
      The sanitization log and photo proof are part of the service, not a separate add-on. Hand the log to a health inspector on demand. See [quality and compliance](/commercial/quality-and-compliance).
    </Note>

    **Best for:** restaurants, cafés, and any food-service space that needs inspection-ready paperwork.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Retail">
    **Every visit:**
    - Storefront glass and entrance
    - Sales-floor vacuum, sweep, and mop
    - Fitting-room reset
    - Restroom sanitization
    - Stockroom sweep and organize
    - Trash, recycling, and cardboard
    - Display dusting (no merchandise contact)

    <Tip>
      We dust around your displays but never handle merchandise. Your stock stays exactly where your team placed it.
    </Tip>

    **Best for:** shops, boutiques, and showrooms. All three tiers fit.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Medical / dental">
    For clinics we recommend the **Premium** tier. The documentation and disinfection are what most medical and dental spaces want.

    **Every visit:**
    - Exam-room turnover sanitization
    - Reception and waiting-area deep clean
    - Medical-grade restroom
    - Sharps-area floor and surfaces
    - Glass and signage
    - Colour-coded mop and cloth system
    - Per-visit auditable sanitization log

    Crews follow PIDAC infection-prevention guidance and OSHA bloodborne-pathogen protocols, with a colour-coded system that keeps tools separated by zone (red for restroom, blue for exam, green for reception).

    **Best for:** medical and dental clinics, physiotherapy, and any space that needs an auditable paper trail. See [quality and compliance](/commercial/quality-and-compliance).
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Salons / spas">
    **Every visit:**
    - Reception reset
    - Treatment and styling-room turnover
    - Hair-station deep clean
    - Hair and product-debris vacuum
    - Restroom sanitization
    - Towel and linen handling
    - Smudge-free mirrors
    - Chemical-bottle disposal

    **Best for:** salons, spas, and barbershops. All three tiers fit; many choose Professional for the extra detail.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
