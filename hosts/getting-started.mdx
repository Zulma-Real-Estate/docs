Zulma for Hosts handles the turnover between guest stays for your Airbnb, Vrbo, or Booking.com rental. Automatically. You link your booking calendar once, and Zulma creates a turnover for each checkout, dispatches a vetted, insured Pro with a pre-assigned backup, and sends before/after photo proof so the unit is guest-ready the same day. One Pro owns the whole turn.

This page walks you through getting set up. For how a turn actually runs, see [how turnovers work](/hosts/how-turnovers-work); for the full picture, start with the [hosts overview](/hosts/overview).

<Info>
  At launch, Zulma for Hosts serves **Niagara Falls** and **Niagara-on-the-Lake**.
</Info>

## What you'll need before you start

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Your calendar link">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Property details">
    Beds, baths, square footage, default check-in/out times, and access instructions (code, lockbox, or key).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Laundry setup (if you want it)">
    For our wash tiers: an in-unit washer/dryer and at least 3 par sets of linen. More on this below.

  <Card title="Authority to list">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Setting up, step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your property">
    Tell us the basics. Address, beds, baths, and square footage. Your exact per-turn price appears as soon as the property is added, so there are no surprises. Adding more than one rental? Multiple properties live under one account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your booking calendar">
    Paste your rental's **read-only iCal link**. Zulma polls it every 30–60 minutes, detects each checkout, and automatically flags same-day turns (a guest checking out and another checking in the same day). You never share your booking-site password. PMS integrations such as Hospitable are coming later. ICal works for everyone today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your property profile">
    Fill in beds, baths, square footage, whether there's in-unit laundry, your par-set count, your default check-in/out times, and your access instructions. Check-in/out times come from this profile, not from the calendar. Your **access instructions are stored encrypted**. Only the assigned Pro sees what they need for the turn.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign your attestations">
    Confirm three things: you have authority to list the property, you hold any required municipal STR licence, and you carry adequate property/STR insurance. This keeps everyone protected and the service above-board.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved">
    A real person reviews your property and attestations. Once you're approved, your card is authorized for the first turn.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your first turn is authorized">
    From here, Zulma generates turnovers on a **rolling 4–6 week** window from your calendar. Each checkout becomes a scheduled turn, dispatched to a Pro with a backup already lined up. Guest-ready the same day, with photo proof.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choosing a laundry level

Laundry is optional and host-owned. You supply the linen, Zulma supplies none. Pick the level that matches your setup.

| Level | What happens | Requirements |
|---|---|---|
| **L1, Turnover only** | We clean the unit; you handle linen. | None |
| **L2, Turnover + Wash** | Beds made guest-ready from your clean spare par-set; the used set is washed, with a photo of the dryer running. Open-loop, no-blame handoff. | **In-unit washer/dryer + 3 par sets** |
| **L3, Turnover + Guaranteed** | The Pro stays the full cycle. Wash, dry, fold, store, photographed. All on the same visit (closed-loop). | In-unit washer/dryer |

<Note>
  L2 and L3 both need an in-unit washer/dryer. L2 also needs **3 par sets** of linen so there's always a clean set on the bed while the used one is in the wash. You can see exact laundry add-on pricing on the [hosts pricing page](/hosts/pricing).
</Note>

## A note on insurance

The **Pro assigned to your turn carries their own \$2M commercial general liability** coverage. So you don't need to carry CGL for the cleaning itself.

<Warning>
  Most STR and host policies. Including AirCover-style coverage. Typically respond only **while a guest is checked in**, not during the turnover window between stays. Check your own policy so you know what's covered when no guest is in the unit.
</Warning>

## The fine print, stated plainly

We'd rather be clear about this up front than bury it.

If a clean is missed or the unit isn't guest-ready, **Zulma's liability is capped at the cleaning fee**. Consequential losses. Guest refunds, relocation costs, lost nights. Are not covered, and standard force-majeure terms apply.

If something isn't right with a completed turn, tell us and we'll make it right with a re-clean or a Zulma credit.

## Pricing at a glance

Your turnover price scales with property size, and you see your exact number the moment you add a property. Commitment matters too. More turns per month means a lower price per turn, and that saving is shared with the Pro.

| Cadence | Turns / month | Per-turn discount |
|---|---|---|
| One-off | 1–3 | 0% |
| Weekly | 4–5 | 12% |
| 2× / week | 6–9 | 20% |
| Near-daily | 10+ | 28% |

A **same-day surge of +25%** applies when a checkout and the next check-in fall on the same calendar day. That's the tightest window to turn around. For the full breakdown of turnover levels, laundry add-ons, and worked examples, see [hosts pricing](/hosts/pricing).
