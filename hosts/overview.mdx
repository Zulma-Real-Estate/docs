Zulma for Hosts is turnover cleaning for short-term rentals, run on autopilot. Link your booking calendar once, and every guest checkout becomes a scheduled turnover automatically. One vetted, insured Pro owns the whole turn, a backup is pre-assigned, and you get before/after photo proof so the unit is guest-ready the same day.

<Info>
**Service area at launch:** Niagara Falls and Niagara-on-the-Lake.
</Info>

## What it is

You host on Airbnb, Vrbo, or Booking.com. You need the place cleaned and reset between every stay, reliably, without chasing anyone. Zulma for Hosts handles that end to end.

- **Calendar-driven.** Connect your listing's read-only calendar and Zulma creates a turnover for each checkout. No password sharing, no manual booking each time.
- **One Pro, one turn.** A single vetted, insured Pro owns the entire turnover. No cross-Pro handoffs mid-clean. A backup Pro is assigned ahead of time in case anything comes up.
- **Photo proof, every time.** You get before/after photos so you can confirm the unit is guest-ready without driving over.
- **Same-day ready.** When a guest checks out and another checks in the same day, the turn is flagged and handled within the tight window.

## Why Zulma

Guaranteed and insured, never auctioned. Your turn isn't thrown to the lowest bidder in a marketplace race-to-the-bottom. It's assigned to a background-checked Pro who carries their own commercial liability insurance, with a backup standing by and photo proof on delivery.

## How it works at a glance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your property">
    Enter beds, baths, square footage, in-unit laundry, par-set count, default check-in/out times, and access instructions. Details are stored encrypted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your calendar">
    Paste your listing's read-only iCal link from Airbnb, Vrbo, or Booking.com. No account password needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the attestations">
    Confirm you have authority to list the property, any required municipal STR licence, and adequate property/STR insurance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved and go live">
    Our team reviews and approves your property, then authorizes your card for the first turn. Turnovers are generated on a rolling 4–6 week window from there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For the full walkthrough, see [How turnovers work](/hosts/how-turnovers-work) and [Getting started](/hosts/getting-started).

## What it costs

Turnover pricing scales with property size, so you see your exact price when you add a property. There are two levels, **Standard turnover** and **Deep turnover** (a heavier reset, roughly 1.4× standard). As an example, a 2-bed Standard turnover runs about **$129** one-off.

The more turns you commit to, the lower the per-turn price. And that saving is shared with the Pro.

| Cadence | Turns / month | Per-turn discount |
|---|---|---|
| One-off | 1–3 | 0% |
| Weekly | 4–5 | 12% |
| 2× / week | 6–9 | 20% |
| Near-daily | 10+ | 28% |

A **same-day surge** of +25% applies when a checkout and the next check-in fall on the same calendar day, because the window is tight.

Add laundry if you want it. From clean-only at no extra cost up to fully guaranteed wash-dry-fold on the same visit. See [Pricing](/hosts/pricing) and [Laundry tiers](/hosts/laundry-tiers) for the full breakdown.

## Insurance, plainly

<Warning>
The **Pro carries their own $2M commercial general liability** insurance, so you don't need your own CGL for the turnover. But know that most STR/host policies (including AirCover-style coverage) typically respond only **while a guest is checked in**. Not during the turnover window. Verify your own coverage.
</Warning>

## The fine print

We'd rather you read this here than be surprised later. If a clean is missed or the unit isn't guest-ready, Zulma's liability is **capped at the cleaning fee**. Consequential losses. Guest refunds, relocation costs, lost nights. Are excluded, and standard force-majeure applies.

If something isn't right with a completed turn, tell us and we'll make it right with a re-clean or a Zulma credit.

<Note>
Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch. Your price is all-in.
</Note>
