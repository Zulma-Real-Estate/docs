Turnover pricing is simple: a base price for the clean, scaled by property size, with a discount the more often you book. Same-day turns add a small surge, and laundry is an optional add-on on top. You see your exact price the moment you add a property. No quote needed for a standard turnover.

<Info>
  Pricing here is for **Zulma for Hosts** (short-term-rental turnovers), live in **Niagara Falls** and **Niagara-on-the-Lake** at launch. New to the service? Start with [How turnovers work](/hosts/how-turnovers-work).
</Info>

## Two turnover levels

Every turnover includes a full clean and before/after photo proof. The level sets how heavy the reset is.

| Level | What it's for | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard turnover** | The usual guest-to-guest reset | Base price |
| **Deep turnover** | A heavier reset. Extra build-up, longer gaps between guests | **≈ 1.4× Standard** |

A Deep turnover runs about **1.4 times** the Standard price for the same property.

## Price scales with property size

The turnover base is set by your property's size. More bedrooms, bathrooms, and square footage mean a higher base. You don't estimate it: when you add a property and fill in its profile, Zulma calculates the exact figure for you.

<Note>
  As a reference point, a **2-bedroom Standard turnover is around \$129** as a one-off. Your own number depends on your property's full profile. Add the property to see it.
</Note>

## Commitment pricing. More turns, lower per-turn price

The more turnovers you book per month, the lower your per-turn price. The saving is shared with the Pro, so frequent turns stay fair on both sides.

| Cadence | Turns / month | Per-turn discount |
|---|---|---|
| One-off | 1–3 | **0%** |
| Weekly | 4–5 | **12%** |
| 2× / week | 6–9 | **20%** |
| Near-daily | 10+ | **28%** |

Here's how that looks for the 2-bedroom Standard example above:

| Cadence | Discount | Per-turn price |
|---|---|---|
| One-off | 0% | **~\$129** |
| Weekly | 12% | **~\$114** |
| 2× / week | 20% | **~\$103** |
| Near-daily | 28% | **~\$93** |

The discount applies automatically based on how often the property turns over. There's no code to enter.

## Same-day surge

When a guest checks out and another checks in on the **same calendar day**, that turn carries a tight window and adds a **+25% surge**. Zulma flags these same-day turns automatically from your calendar, so you'll know before they run.

<Tip>
  Building in a buffer day between bookings avoids the same-day surge. But when back-to-back is unavoidable, the +25% covers the squeeze of getting the unit guest-ready in hours, not a full day.
</Tip>

## Laundry add-on

Laundry is optional and priced on top of the turnover. There are three levels. Clean only, washed open-loop, or a full stay-and-guarantee cycle. You own the linen at every level; Zulma supplies none.

| Level | Name | Added cost |
|---|---|---|
| **L1** | Turnover only | **+\$0** |
| **L2** | Turnover + Wash | **+\$12–\$30** by size |
| **L3** | Turnover + Guaranteed | **adds ~\$28–\$55** by size (3–4BR by quote) |

See [Host laundry options](/hosts/laundry-tiers) for exactly what each level includes and what your property needs.

## Putting it together

Your final per-turn price is built like this:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with the base">
    The turnover base for your property's size, at the Standard rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the level">
    Keep Standard, or use Deep (≈ 1.4× Standard) for a heavier reset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply your commitment discount">
    0% to 28% off, based on how many turns the property runs per month.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add same-day surge if it applies">
    +25% only on turns where checkout and the next check-in fall on the same day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add laundry if you've chosen it">
    L1 (+\$0), L2 (+\$12–\$30), or L3 (~\$28–\$55), on top of the turn.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You don't have to do this maths. **Add a property and you'll see your exact price** before anything is charged. Your card is only authorized once your property is approved.
</Info>

## Good to know

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do I see my exact price?">
    As soon as you add a property and complete its profile (beds, baths, square footage, laundry setup). The price is calculated from that profile, so there's no quote step for a standard turnover.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does the commitment discount get applied?">
    Automatically, based on how often your property turns over each month. You don't enter a code. Book more turns and the per-turn price drops to the right tier (12% / 20% / 28%).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What triggers the same-day surge?">
    A guest checking out and another checking in on the **same calendar day**. Zulma detects this from your calendar and flags the turn before it runs. If there's a buffer day between bookings, no surge applies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is tax added on top?">
    No. Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch. The turnover price you see is all-in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a turn isn't right?">
    Tell us and we'll make it right with a re-clean or a Zulma credit. The full turnover guarantee and its limits are covered in [How turnovers work](/hosts/how-turnovers-work).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
