Zulma is an on-demand platform that connects people across the Niagara region of Ontario with vetted, insured Pros for cleaning, lawn care, car detailing, and more. You pick the service, choose a time slot, and Zulma matches you with an available Pro, often in under a minute. Prices are all-in. The number you see at booking is the number you pay, with no contracts and no surprises.

## What Zulma offers

Zulma covers your home, your yard, your vehicle, and your business through several service lines.

**Residential cleaning** handles everything inside your home across four tiers:

- **Standard Clean** for regular upkeep (2–3 hrs, from $219)
- **Deep Clean** for seasonal or first-time top-to-bottom work (4–6 hrs, from $315)
- **Move-in / Move-out** for an empty home in transition (5–7 hrs, from $347)
- **Post-Construction Clean** to clear dust and debris after a reno or build (6–8 hrs, from $429)

**Lawn care** keeps the outside sharp:

- **Mowing** with cut, edge, and tidy, weekly or biweekly (from $49)
- **Hedge Trimming** to shape shrubs and hedges to spec (from $89)
- **Spring Cleanup** with rake, debris removal, edging, and first cut (from $199)
- **Fall Cleanup** with leaf removal and end-of-season prep (from $249)

**Car detailing** is mobile, so the Pro comes to you:

- **Express Wash** for a quick refresh (from $55)
- **Full Wash** inside and out (from $150)
- **Detail** for a deep, paint-and-interior reset (from $300)

**Commercial cleaning** keeps offices, retail, salons, medical and dental spaces, and restaurants clean on a schedule that fits your business. Pricing is per square foot by industry, with Essential, Professional, and Premium tiers. [Get a quote](/commercial/getting-a-quote) for your space.

**Zulma for Hosts** is automated short-term-rental turnover cleaning. Link your booking calendar and Zulma creates a turnover after every checkout, dispatches a vetted, insured Pro with a pre-assigned backup, and delivers before and after photo proof so the unit is guest-ready the same day. See [how turnovers work](/hosts/how-turnovers-work).

<Note>
  **Laundry** (wash, dry, and fold with pickup and delivery) is coming soon to Niagara. Tap **Remind me** in the app to join the waitlist and we will let you know the moment it goes live. Learn more on the [laundry](/services/laundry) page.
</Note>

## Why Zulma

Zulma was built to solve a gap in the Niagara market: reliable home and property services with clear pricing, vetted Pros, and real accountability behind every booking.

- **Vetted and insured Pros.** Every Pro is background-checked and identity-verified before taking jobs, and carries their own liability insurance. Zulma itself carries a $5,000,000 Commercial General Liability policy and $100,000 cyber coverage.
- **Transparent pricing.** The price you see at booking is the price you pay. Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch, so your price is all-in with no hidden fees.
- **Fast matching.** Book a slot and Zulma matches you with an available Pro, often in under a minute in the core service area.
- **No contracts.** Recurring schedules can be paused, skipped, or cancelled anytime from your account.
- **Made right when it isn't.** If something is missed, report it within 24 hours and Zulma will arrange a re-clean of those areas or a Zulma credit. Our remedy is to fix it or credit your account, not to leave it.

<Info>
  Zulma is operated by Zulma Resources Inc., a federally incorporated company operating in Ontario as Zulma Real Estate. Payments run through Stripe, so Zulma never sees your full card number.
</Info>
