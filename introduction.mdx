Zulma is an on-demand platform that connects homeowners in the Niagara region of Ontario with vetted, insured crew members for residential cleaning and lawn care. You pick the service, choose a time slot, and Zulma matches you with an available crew member — often in under 30 seconds. Every booking comes with transparent, HST-included pricing and no contracts.

## What Zulma offers

Zulma covers both sides of home maintenance through two service categories.

**Cleaning** handles everything inside your home across four tiers:

- **Standard Clean** — regular upkeep for a fresh home (2–3 hrs, from $169)
- **Deep Clean** — top-to-bottom, every corner (4–6 hrs, from $256)
- **Move-in / Move-out** — complete clean for transitions (5–7 hrs, from $297)
- **Post-Construction Clean** — remove dust and debris after a build (6–8 hrs, from $338)

**Lawn care** covers the outside across four services:

- **Weekly Mowing** — cut, edge, and tidy, every other week or weekly (0.75–1.5 hrs, from $49)
- **Spring Cleanup** — rake, debris removal, edging, and first cut of season (3–5 hrs, from $199)
- **Fall Cleanup** — leaf removal, gutter cleaning, end-of-season prep (4–6 hrs, from $249)
- **Hedge Trimming** — shape and trim shrubs and hedges to spec (1.5–3 hrs, from $89)

<Note>
  Lawn care is seasonal, typically available April through November in the Niagara region.
</Note>

## Why Zulma

Zulma was built to solve a gap in the Niagara market: reliable home services with clear pricing, vetted crew, and real accountability behind every booking.

- **Vetted and insured crew** — every crew member is reference-checked and covered by Zulma's $2,000,000 Commercial General Liability policy.
- **Transparent pricing** — the price you see at booking is the price you pay. HST (13%) is itemized on every quote before you confirm. No hidden fees.
- **Same-day availability** — book a slot for today when crew members are online. Average match time is under 30 seconds in the core service area.
- **No contracts** — recurring schedules can be paused or cancelled anytime.
- **Satisfaction guarantee** — if something is missed, report it within 24 hours and Zulma will send someone back to re-clean those areas at no charge.

<Info>
  Zulma is operated by Zulma Resources Inc., a federally incorporated Ontario corporation. A $2M commercial liability policy and HST registration are in place on every booking.
</Info>
