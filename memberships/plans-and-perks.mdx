Zulma has two memberships. Both turn everyday bookings into a little money back, smooth out the small fees, and put your favourite Pros within reach first. Pick the one that fits how often you book.

<Info>
Current pricing and checkout live at [app.zulma.ca/account/membership](https://app.zulma.ca/account/membership). The numbers below are the published plan prices; the app always shows what's current.
</Info>

## The two tiers at a glance

| | Zulma+ | Zulma Household |
|---|---|---|
| **Price** | **\$9.99/month or \$99/year** | **\$24.99/month or \$249/year** |
| Cashback (as Zulma credit) | +2% on every booking | +3% on every booking |
| Tip protection | up to \$10/quarter, 4× a year | up to \$10/quarter, 4× a year |
| Free same-day rush | yes | yes |
| Cancellation grace | late-cancel fee waived | late-cancel fee waived |
| First-look priority on favourite Pros | yes | yes |
| Quarterly household credit | None | **\$50 every quarter** |

Zulma Household includes everything in Zulma+, then adds the higher cashback rate and the \$50 quarterly credit.

## What each perk means

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cashback as Zulma credit">
    A percentage of every booking comes back to you as Zulma credit, **+2%** on Zulma+, **+3%** on Zulma Household. Credit applies automatically to future bookings; it isn't a cash payout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tip protection">
    Covers up to **\$10 per quarter**, four times a year. Your Pro is always paid in full. This perk protects your side of the tip.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Free same-day rush">
    The same-day rush surcharge is waived on your bookings, so a last-minute clean doesn't cost extra.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Extended cancellation grace">
    The 1–2-hour late-cancel fee is waived for members. See the full tiers in our [cancellation policy](/booking/cancellation).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="First-look priority on favourite Pros">
    A short priority window on the Pros you've favourited, so the people who already know your home get the first chance to take the job.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="\$50 household credit (Household only)">
    Zulma Household adds **\$50 in Zulma credit every quarter**. On top of cashback. Use it toward any booking.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Joining and cancelling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Open [app.zulma.ca/account/membership](https://app.zulma.ca/account/membership) and pick Zulma+ or Zulma Household, monthly or yearly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start earning right away">
    Cashback, free rush, and your perks apply from your next booking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Changed your mind?">
    Cancel within **14 days** for a full refund of the membership fee. After that, your membership runs through the period you've paid for.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch. Your membership price is all-in.
</Note>
