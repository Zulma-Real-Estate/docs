Zulma dispatches crew members across the Niagara region of Ontario and into Hamilton. Most weekday bookings in the core areas match in under two minutes. Same-day availability and lawn care scheduling vary by municipality and time of year.

<Note>
  Same-day availability varies by municipality. Core areas like St. Catharines, Thorold, and Niagara Falls typically have the most same-day slots. Availability in outer areas depends on crew coverage at the time you book.
</Note>

## Lawn care availability

Lawn care services — mowing, spring cleanup, fall cleanup, and hedge trimming — are seasonal. Service is typically available from April through November in the Niagara region.

<Info>
  Lawn care visits are weather-dependent. Zulma or your assigned crew member may reschedule a visit at no charge when weather makes the work unsafe or ineffective (active rain, lightning, wet grass, ice, etc.). You'll be notified as soon as conditions develop, and rescheduled to the next available day in your window.
</Info>

## Noise bylaws for lawn care

Most Niagara-region municipalities restrict gas-powered or amplified equipment to standard daytime hours, typically 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. on weekdays. Crew members will not arrive before the earliest permitted local start time. If your municipality enforces stricter rules, add a note to your booking and Zulma will route accordingly.
