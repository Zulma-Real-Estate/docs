Zulma serves the Niagara region of Ontario and into Hamilton. Most weekday bookings in the core areas match in under two minutes. Same-day availability and lawn care scheduling vary by city and time of year.

<Tip>
  The fastest way to confirm coverage is to enter your address in the app at [app.zulma.ca](https://app.zulma.ca). You'll get an instant yes or no for your exact location, plus live pricing and open slots.
</Tip>

## Cities we serve

Zulma currently serves these ten cities:

- St. Catharines
- Niagara Falls
- Niagara-on-the-Lake
- Thorold
- Welland
- Fort Erie
- Grimsby
- Lincoln
- Pelham

Residential cleaning, lawn care, and car detailing are available across all ten. Commercial cleaning covers all ten as well. See [getting a commercial quote](/commercial/getting-a-quote) for B2B coverage.

<Note>
  Same-day availability varies by city. Core areas like St. Catharines, Thorold, and Niagara Falls typically have the most same-day slots. Availability in outer areas depends on Pro coverage at the time you book.
</Note>

## Short-term-rental turnovers

[Zulma for Hosts](/hosts/overview) handles automated turnover cleaning between guest stays for Airbnb, Vrbo, and Booking.com hosts. At launch, turnovers are available in **Niagara Falls and Niagara-on-the-Lake**. We're expanding to more of the region next.

## Laundry

Wash-dry-fold [laundry](/services/laundry) is **coming soon** across all ten cities. It isn't bookable yet. Open the laundry tile in the app and tap **Remind me** to join the waitlist, and we'll let you know the moment it goes live in your area.

## Lawn care availability

Lawn care services, including mowing, spring cleanup, fall cleanup, and hedge trimming, are seasonal. Service is typically available from April through November in the Niagara region.

<Info>
  Lawn care visits are weather-dependent. Zulma or your assigned Pro may reschedule a visit at no charge when weather makes the work unsafe or ineffective (active rain, lightning, wet grass, ice, and similar conditions). You'll be notified as soon as conditions develop, and rescheduled to the next available day in your window.
</Info>

## Noise bylaws for lawn care

Most Niagara-region cities restrict gas-powered or amplified equipment to standard daytime hours, typically 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. on weekdays. Pros will not arrive before the earliest permitted local start time. If your city enforces stricter rules, add a note to your booking and Zulma will route accordingly.
