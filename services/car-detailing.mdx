Zulma car detailing is **fully mobile**. You pick a time, and a vetted Pro drives to your home or office with everything they need. No drop-off, no waiting room, no detour.

There are three tiers, from a quick exterior refresh to a deep multi-hour reset. The price you see at booking is the price you pay.

## The three tiers

| Tier | From (sedan) | Best for | Typical time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Express Wash** | **\$55** | A fast exterior refresh between deeper cleans | 0.4–0.6 hr |
| **Full Wash** | **\$150** | Inside-and-out clean for everyday upkeep | 1–1.5 hr |
| **Detail (deep)** | **\$300** | A complete reset. Interior and exterior, top to bottom | 3–5 hr |

Prices above are starting prices for a sedan. Your exact quote depends on your vehicle size and any add-ons. You'll see the final number before you confirm.

<Info>
Express Wash and Full Wash can be booked as **recurring** visits to keep your vehicle in shape year-round. The deep Detail is a one-time service.
</Info>

## How vehicle size affects price

When you book, you identify your vehicle through the built-in vehicle database, and the price scales to its size:

| Vehicle size | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Compact | ~0.8× |
| Sedan | 1.0× (baseline) |
| SUV or truck | ~1.3× |

So a Full Wash that starts at \$150 for a sedan scales up for an SUV or truck, and down for a compact.

## Condition surcharges

If a vehicle needs extra work to bring it back to clean, a condition surcharge keeps pricing fair to the Pro doing the work. These are flagged transparently:

| Condition | Surcharge |
|---|---|
| Pet hair | \$25 |
| Mud / off-road | \$20 |
| Smoker / odour | \$30 |
| Heavy stains | \$30 |

## How to book

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Go to [app.zulma.ca](https://app.zulma.ca) and choose **Car detailing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify your vehicle">
    Look up your vehicle in the built-in database so pricing matches its size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a tier and add-ons">
    Choose Express, Full, or Detail, then add any extras. The calculator shows your exact total as you go.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a time and place">
    Tell us where the vehicle will be. Your home or office. And when. The Pro comes to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review your all-in price and book. For Express or Full, you can set it to repeat on a schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For a full walkthrough of the booking flow, see [How to book](/booking/how-to-book).

<Note>
Detailing is mobile-only. The Pro needs reasonable access to the vehicle (a driveway, parking spot, or similar). There's no shop to drop the car off at.
</Note>
