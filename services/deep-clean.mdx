The deep clean is Zulma's most thorough residential service. Your cleaner works through every room covering everything in a standard clean, then goes further — wiping every baseboard, detailing blinds and tracks, cleaning ceiling fans, opening cabinets, and pulling out appliances. Plan for 4–6 hours. Pricing starts at $256 CAD; your final price with HST is shown before you confirm, with no hidden fees.

<Info>
  Available 7 days a week across the Niagara region and Hamilton. Same-day bookings are matched in under 30 seconds on average.
</Info>

## What's included

Every item below is part of the base price. A deep clean starts with the full standard scope, then adds the detailed work.

**Full standard clean of every room**
- Beds made, surfaces dusted, floors vacuumed and mopped
- Kitchen and bathrooms fully serviced (counters, appliances exterior, sink, faucet, toilet, tub/shower, mirrors)
- Microwave interior wiped

**Deep clean additions**
- Baseboards — wiped throughout the home
- Blinds and window tracks — dusted and detailed
- Ceiling fans — blades and housing wiped
- Inside cabinets — shelves and interiors wiped
- Behind appliances — pulled out where movable, floor cleaned underneath
- Light fixtures — wiped and polished

## What's not included

<Note>
  The following are out of scope for a deep clean. Most can be added at booking time.

  - Inside fridge or oven (popular add-ons)
  - Interior window glass (popular add-on)
  - Carpet steam cleaning (separate specialist recommended)

## When to choose a deep clean

**Spring or fall reset** — Get the house in pristine shape twice a year so your routine standard cleans stay manageable.

**Before hosting** — Holiday parties, in-laws visiting, a baby shower. Book it 2–3 days before so the place feels presented.

**First clean with a new cleaner** — Setting a high baseline makes recurring standard cleans much more effective.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often should I book a deep clean?">
    Twice a year is typical — once in spring and once in fall. If you have pets, allergies, or a busy household, every 3–4 months keeps things in great shape.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does it take so much longer than a standard clean?">
    Deep cleans are physically intensive — pulling cushions, wiping every baseboard, detailing blinds and tracks. Zulma allocates 4–6 hours so the job is done right, not rushed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to move things out of the way?">
    Not really — but clearing clutter off counters and floors helps the cleaner reach the surfaces themselves. Large furniture won't be moved for safety reasons.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add inside-fridge or oven cleaning?">
    Yes — they're popular add-ons at $30 and $40 respectively. Pick them at booking time. The cleaner will pre-treat surfaces while working on other areas for the most thorough result.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between a deep clean and a move-in/out clean?">
    A move-in/out clean is a deep clean of an empty home — no furniture in the way means the cleaner can get behind everything and inside every cabinet without obstacles. A deep clean is for an occupied, furnished home.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
