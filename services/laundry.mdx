## How it will work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a pickup window">
    Choose a 2-hour window. Same-day pickup is planned if you book before noon. Contactless is fine. Just leave your bag out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We weigh it at pickup">
    Your Pro weighs the bag and photographs the scale. Your price is the **actual weight**. Never more than your estimate without your okay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We wash it on its own">
    Each customer's laundry is washed on its own, **never mixed with anyone else's**. We follow your saved preferences for temperature, drying, and detergent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It comes back folded">
    Clean, folded, and bagged, then delivered to your door. If you're not in, we'll leave it with a photo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How weighing and payment will work

You don't have to guess the weight. We price on what your laundry actually weighs, and we never quietly charge more than you expected.

<Steps>
  <Step title="We weigh and photograph at pickup">
    Your Pro weighs your bag and snaps a photo of the scale, so the number is never a mystery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Well over your estimate? We ask first">
    If your laundry weighs in well over what you estimated, we **pause and message you** with the new weight and price before we wash a thing. No surprise charges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're charged on the real weight">
    Your final price is based on the actual weight plus any add-ons. All itemized on your receipt, with no sales tax during launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Care & peace of mind

- **Your laundry, on its own**. One customer per machine cycle. We never mix loads.
- **Saved preferences**. Set your temperature, drying, and detergent once; we remember them.
- **Fragrance-free on request**. Flag a sensitivity and we'll wash fragrance-free.
- **Photos as proof**. We weigh, wash, and deliver with photos along the way.

<Note>
  Spot a problem after delivery? Tell us within **24 hours** and we'll make it right with a re-wash or a Zulma credit.
</Note>
