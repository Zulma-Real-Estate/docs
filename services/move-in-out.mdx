The move-in / move-out clean is designed for empty homes going through a transition: tenants leaving, new buyers moving in, or landlords turning over a unit. With furniture out of the way, your Pro gets inside every cabinet and drawer, behind every appliance, and into every baseboard and blind. Most empty homes are completed in 5–7 hours. Pricing starts at $347. Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch, so your price is all-in: the number you see before you confirm is the number you pay, with no hidden fees.

<Info>
  Available 7 days a week across the Niagara region and Hamilton. For weekend or holiday move dates, book at least a week ahead. Those slots fill fast.
</Info>

## What's included

Every item below is part of the base price. This scope assumes the home is empty.

**Kitchen**
- Inside fridge and freezer: degreased, sanitized, and smell-neutralized
- Inside oven and range hood
- Inside dishwasher and microwave
- Behind major appliances: pulled out where movable, floor cleaned underneath
- Garbage disposal: cleaned and run with citrus
- Inside cabinets, drawers, and pantry

**Bathrooms**
- Full bathroom detail: toilet, tub/shower, sink, mirrors, fixtures
- Inside vanity cabinets and drawers

**Throughout the home**
- All baseboards: wiped throughout
- All blinds: dusted and detailed
- All light fixtures: wiped and polished
- Floors vacuumed and mopped in every room
- Closets cleaned out

## What's not included

<Note>
  The following are out of scope for a move-in / move-out clean.

  - Carpet steam cleaning (separate carpet specialist recommended)
  - Wall painting or hole patching
  - Outdoor garage or shed (available as add-on)
  - Lawn or yard work
</Note>

## Who this service is for

**Tenants protecting their deposit.** Most Ontario landlords expect this level of clean for a full deposit return. Zulma provides a detailed receipt you can share with your landlord or property manager.

**New homebuyers.** Move into a place that smells and feels like new, especially after the previous owners' last frantic clean.

**Landlords between tenants.** Get the unit photo-ready for listings or showings within 24–48 hours of move-out.
