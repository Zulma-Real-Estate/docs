Zulma offers cleaning, lawn care, and car detailing across the Niagara region of Ontario, served by the same vetted, background-checked Pros. Pros are identity-verified, pass a criminal record check before taking any job, and carry their own liability insurance. Zulma carries $5M commercial general liability plus $100K cyber coverage. Prices below are starting prices, and they are all-in: Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch, so the number you see is the number you pay.
