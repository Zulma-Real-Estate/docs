The standard clean is Zulma's most popular service — routine maintenance that keeps your home consistently fresh without the effort. A vetted cleaner works through your kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms, and living areas in 2–3 hours, wiping surfaces, scrubbing the sink, vacuuming, and mopping. Pricing starts at $169 CAD; your final price with HST is shown before you confirm, with no hidden fees.

## What's included

Every item below is part of the base price. No upsells on arrival.

**Kitchen**
- Counters and stovetop — wiped with eco-safe products, polished to a shine
- Exterior of appliances — front of fridge, dishwasher, microwave, oven
- Sink and faucet — scrubbed, descaled, polished
- Microwave interior — wiped and refreshed
- Floor — swept, vacuumed, and mopped

**Bathrooms**
- Toilet, tub/shower, and sink scrubbed and sanitized
- Mirrors and fixtures polished
- Floor vacuumed and mopped

**Bedrooms and living areas**
- Surfaces dusted and wiped
- Beds made
- Floors vacuumed and mopped

## When to choose a standard clean

**Recurring upkeep** — Weekly or biweekly cleans keep your home consistently fresh. Zulma offers 15–20% off when you set up a recurring schedule.

**Busy households** — Two-income families or parents who'd rather spend the weekend with their kids than scrubbing bathrooms.

**Between deep cleans** — A standard clean maintains the work of a deep clean, so you only need the thorough one every 3–6 months.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often should I book a standard clean?">
    Most customers go biweekly — that's the sweet spot for keeping a home looking fresh without overspending. Weekly works well for larger families or homes with pets and kids. Monthly is doable but you'll likely need a deep clean every 3–4 months on top.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to be home?">
    No. Most customers leave a lockbox code, smart-lock PIN, or doorman instructions in their booking. Zulma shares access details with the cleaner only after they accept the job.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will it be the same cleaner each time?">
    If you favourite a cleaner and book recurring, Zulma prioritizes them when they're available. If they're booked or sick, you're matched with another vetted cleaner — and you can always reschedule free up to 24 hours before.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What products do you use?">
    Cleaners default to eco-friendly, fragrance-free supplies safe for kids and pets. If you have specific products you want used, leave a note in your booking and the cleaner will follow your instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if something gets missed?">
    Report it within 24 hours and Zulma will send someone back to re-clean those areas free of charge. The 100% satisfaction guarantee is built into every clean.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
