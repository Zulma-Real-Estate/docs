The standard clean is Zulma's most popular service: routine maintenance that keeps your home consistently fresh without the effort. A vetted cleaner works through your kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms, and living areas in 2–3 hours, wiping surfaces, scrubbing the sink, vacuuming, and mopping. Pricing starts from $219, and the all-in price is shown before you confirm, with no hidden fees. Zulma isn't charging sales tax during launch, so the number you see is the number you pay.

<Info>
  Available 7 days a week across the Niagara region and Hamilton. Same-day bookings are matched in under 30 seconds on average.
</Info>

## What's included

Every item below is part of the base price. No upsells on arrival.

**Kitchen**
- Counters and stovetop: wiped with eco-safe products, polished to a shine
- Exterior of appliances: front of fridge, dishwasher, microwave, oven
- Sink and faucet: scrubbed, descaled, polished
- Microwave interior: wiped and refreshed
- Floor: swept, vacuumed, and mopped

**Bathrooms**
- Toilet, tub/shower, and sink scrubbed and sanitized
- Mirrors and fixtures polished
- Floor vacuumed and mopped

**Bedrooms and living areas**
- Surfaces dusted and wiped
- Beds made
- Floors vacuumed and mopped

## What's not included

<Note>
  The following are out of scope for a standard clean. Most can be added at booking time.

  - Inside fridge, oven, or cabinets (available as add-on)
  - Interior windows or blind tracks (available as add-on)
  - Wall washing or scuff removal (available as add-on)
  - Heavy debris removal or moving large furniture
</Note>

## What it costs

The standard clean starts from $219 for a baseline 3-bed, 2-bath home with no add-ons. Larger homes scale up: add $20 per bedroom beyond 3 and $15 per bathroom beyond 2. A few slots carry a surcharge (same-day rush, a peak Friday-evening or weekend slot, or an edge-zone trip), and these are always shown before you confirm. For the exact price on your home, use the live calculator at [app.zulma.ca](https://app.zulma.ca).

## When to choose a standard clean

**Recurring upkeep**: Weekly or biweekly cleans keep your home consistently fresh. Set up a recurring schedule and the discount applies from your second visit: 20% weekly, 15% biweekly, 10% monthly.

**Busy households**: Two-income families or parents who'd rather spend the weekend with their kids than scrubbing bathrooms.

**Between deep cleans**: A standard clean maintains the work of a deep clean, so you only need the thorough one every 3 to 6 months.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often should I book a standard clean?">
    Most customers go biweekly: that's the sweet spot for keeping a home looking fresh without overspending. Weekly works well for larger families or homes with pets and kids. Monthly is doable, but you'll likely want a deep clean every 3 to 4 months on top.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to be home?">
    No. Most customers leave a lockbox code, smart-lock PIN, or doorman instructions in their booking. Zulma shares access details with the cleaner only after they accept the job. You'll get a push and email notification when the Pro arrives and again when the clean is complete.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will it be the same cleaner each time?">
    If you favourite a cleaner and book recurring, Zulma prioritizes them when they're available. If they're booked or unwell, you're matched with another vetted cleaner, and you can always reschedule free up to 24 hours before.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What products do you use?">
    Cleaners default to eco-friendly, fragrance-free supplies safe for kids and pets. If you have specific products you want used, leave a note in your booking and the cleaner will follow your instructions. You can also request the green, unscented line at booking for a small premium.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if something gets missed?">
    Report it within 24 hours and Zulma will send someone back to re-clean those areas at no charge, or apply a Zulma credit to your account. Quality is backed on every clean.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
