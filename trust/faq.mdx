## Booking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How quickly can I book a same-day clean?">
    Same-day bookings are available when crew members are online. The average accept time in the core Niagara service area is under 30 seconds. A $50 same-day rush fee applies to any booking made less than 24 hours in advance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I request the same cleaner each time?">
    Yes. You can favorite a crew member and set up a recurring schedule — Zulma prioritizes them for your upcoming bookings when they're available. If your preferred crew member isn't available for a particular visit, another vetted crew member is matched, and you can reschedule free of charge up to 24 hours before the booking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to be home during the clean?">
    No. Most customers leave a lockbox code, a smart-lock PIN, or doorman instructions in their booking notes. Access details are only shared with the crew member after they accept the job.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if something gets missed?">
    Tell us within 24 hours and we'll send someone back to re-clean those specific areas at no charge. File a report from your booking history at [account/disputes](https://app.zulma.ca/account/disputes).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payment

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When does my card get charged?">
    Your card is pre-authorized for the quoted amount when you confirm a booking. The charge is only captured once a crew member accepts the job. If no crew member accepts in time, the hold is fully released — no charge.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are tips required?">
    No. Tips are entirely optional. When you add one through the platform, 100% of the tip goes directly to the crew member who did the work. Zulma takes no portion of any tip.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is HST included in the price shown?">
    HST (13%) is itemized and shown in the price breakdown before you confirm. It is not hidden inside the quoted amount. Every receipt issued through the platform is CRA-compliant.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Trust & safety

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are all cleaners background-checked?">
    Yes. Every crew member clears a vulnerable-sector criminal record check, WHMIS 2015 certification, two live reference checks with prior employers or clients, and a shadowed first booking before they take solo jobs. Records are re-run yearly. See the full vetting process at [trust/vetting](/trust/vetting).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if something gets damaged?">
    Report the damage within 48 hours through your booking history at [account/disputes](https://app.zulma.ca/account/disputes). Every booking is covered by Zulma's $2,000,000 Commercial General Liability policy, which covers property damage arising from crew negligence. Pre-existing damage is not covered.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if something goes missing?">
    Report within 24 hours through [account/disputes](https://app.zulma.ca/account/disputes) and also to your local police. Theft by crew members is covered by Zulma's $25,000–$50,000 janitorial bond. See the full coverage details at [trust/insurance](/trust/insurance).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What personal information does Zulma collect?">
    Zulma collects account information (name, email, phone), your service address, booking history, and payment information. Card details are handled by Stripe — Zulma stores only a tokenized reference and never holds raw card numbers. See the full [Privacy Policy](https://app.zulma.ca/legal/privacy) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data sold?">
    No. Zulma does not sell or rent your personal information to anyone, for any purpose.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    Go to [account/settings](https://app.zulma.ca/account/settings) to request account deletion. Note that booking and payment records are retained for seven years as required by the Canada Revenue Agency.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
