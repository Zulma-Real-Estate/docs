Before anyone shows up at your door, they've cleared a six-step process that Zulma runs on every crew member — cleaners and lawn care providers alike. There are no exceptions and no trial runs on real customer homes. Here's exactly what that looks like.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Criminal record check">
    Every crew member undergoes a criminal record check before onboarding. Cleaners who enter private homes are subject to a vulnerable-sector check — the more thorough screening required when someone has unsupervised access to a residence. Records are verified and re-run yearly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="WHMIS 2015 certification">
    Anyone handling commercial cleaning chemicals must hold a current Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System (WHMIS 2015) certification. This covers safe handling, storage, and disposal of hazardous products. Certification is renewed every three years.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insured and bonded">
    Every crew member is covered under Zulma's $2,000,000 Commercial General Liability (CGL) policy, which covers third-party property damage and bodily injury arising from crew negligence on any booking made through the platform. Cleaners who work alone inside homes are also bonded — providing additional protection against theft.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Two reference checks">
    Two prior employers or clients are actually called — not asked to fill in a form. The focus isn't credentials; it's whether this person is reliable and respectful when working inside someone's home.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skills check on a real home">
    A crew member's first booking is shadowed by a senior crew member who observes the work firsthand. They don't take solo bookings until they pass that check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ongoing rating bar">
    Passing the initial vetting isn't enough on its own. Crew members must maintain a minimum 4.7★ rating across their last 20 jobs on the platform. Falling below that threshold results in a pause for retraining — or removal from the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who's coming to your home

When your booking confirms, you're told exactly who to expect: first name, last initial, profile photo, current rating, and the number of homes they've cleaned through Zulma. You can view their full profile before they arrive.
